FIREFIGHTER RESCUE

How to Play

Your mission is to put out fires and save the city!

• Use arrow keys or swipe to move the firefighter

• Spray water on fires to extinguish them

• Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities

• Upgrade your hose to increase water pressure

• Stop the fire from spreading to other buildings
